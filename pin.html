Blue Monarch is a soda beverage made with 100% organic ingredients. The flavors are lemonade, peach, and apple. However, all three beverages are mixed with a flower called Clitoria ternatea, also known as butterfly pea or blue tea. Hence, our brand name is Blue Monarch because it is related to a butterfly and its natural colors.
